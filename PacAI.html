Pacman AI Tournament

CSE 140 Artificial Inteligence Tournament Winners

This class project consisted of making two AI Pacman agents compete in a tournament to beat other team's agents. The tournament ran for a week and we were able to look at the match results after each day to improve our agent, as only the final day decided the standings. We had the option of creating any agent we could within the time limit and we chose to make a reflexive agent for the simplicity of the design of feature and weight pairs. Our team, DrPacmanhattan, won the tournament!

Agent Design

Since each team has two agents different strategies emerge to balance offense and defense. Some teams choose to dedicate their agents to solely attacking or defending. However, we chose to create only one, more versatile reflexive agent that we applied to both our pacmans. A reflexive agent is one that uses the information of the current state of the game to make the best move based on feature and weight pairs. Our reflexive agent's behavior is dictated by different sets of feature & weight pairs that we created. We switch between feature & weight pairs depending on whether the agent is currently a ghost, pacman, or a scared ghost.

Our agent was not one of the most complicated among the teams but I think its one of the reasons we were able to win the tournament. That is not to say we didn't run into any challenges, which we discuss in our final writeup.
